Step 3 — Regime classification

Market_State is discrete.

Typical rule set:

If:

Trend_t > 0 AND Vol_t < median_vol

→ Bull regime

If:

Trend_t < 0 AND Vol_t > median_vol

→ Bear regime

Else

→ Neutral regime

Encoded numerically:

State	Meaning
1	Bull
0	Neutral
-1	Bear
